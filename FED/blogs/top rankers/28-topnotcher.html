FIRST TIME IN PNLE HISTORY: 84 TOPNOTCHERS IN JUST ONE REVIEW ACADEMY!

Nursing Majoha Gomburza

Today, TopRank once again made history by having 84 board topnotchers who were able to reach the top spots in the PNLE. These students were among the 18,529 who passed the November 2022 Philippine Nurse Licensure Examination out of 24,903 who took the board exam.

Congratulations to all our TopRank Topnotchers! We greatly appreciate all your efforts in preparing for this big day. Your hard work, perseverance, learning, studying, sacrifice and most of all, your love for this profession is what made you all victors of this season!

Having this kind of success keeps TopRank motivated to help more students achieve their goal of becoming a licensed professional. They aimed to top the board exam, and they did! We did this all together, our dear TopRankers. WE MADE HISTORY TODAY!
